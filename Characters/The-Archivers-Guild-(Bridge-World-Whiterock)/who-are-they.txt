Practically refugees of the E.S. world.
They recognize the nature of every major organization there, they recognize the actions of the guardians, they understand the trajectory and wish to save themselves.
They were wary of the Bridge world's few federations at firsts, but eventually found themselves inadvertently becoming one of those "guilds" with their equipment and expertise.
They work to salvage the culture of the world and spread it far and wide.
They came with the advanced tech and knowledge of the E.S. world, but they still do discover, among many other valuable things, technological advancements once lost to Beatrice's "judgement day".
They plan on making possible something similar to academia in their new home, but for now they only manufacture "archival tools" that they use, and teach their "crafts" to interested "adventurers".
